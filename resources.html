NEED HELP FINDING HOUSING?

It's important to know that finding affordable housing can take time, and there may be waiting lists for certain programs. Don't hesitate to reach out for assistance and keep looking for housing options that meet your needs and budget. Here are some resources that may be able to assist you.

Are you a student? Living in NYC? Student Housing

Need secure housing? All amenities free? StudentHousingWorks

Apply for housing lotteries? HCR Lotteries

Find housing based on household size and total income. Housing Connect

Apply for NYCHA public housing. NYCHA

Emergency Family Shelters. Nazereth Housing

LGBTQ friendly programs. Heart Mind Haven
